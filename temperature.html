Temperature

Visualizations

As expected, temperatures rise as you get closer to the Equator. However, it seems that they are equally as hot within +/- 20 degrees of latitude. Further, temperatures seem to drop more steeply further north of the Equator than further south. This is probably because of the lack of cities that far south and therefore lack of observable data to plot.
